About Me

Matthew is a former U.S. Navy electronics technician currently studying to be a full stack web developer. He lives in North Park, San Diego with his Wife, Taylor. Originally from Wichita, Kansas, the Navy brought him to San Diego and he fell in love with the city.

Matthew aspires to be a game developer, model hobbyist, cook, and carpenter. He also dreams of home ownership and having an actual parking spot.

His main past time currently is video games. Civilization VI and Subnautica occupy most of his gaming time. He also loves playing tennis.
